Chiken Roast

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, spices, lemon juice, and salt for at least 2 hours.
  2. Preheat oven to 200°C (390°F) or use a stovetop.
  3. Heat oil or butter and sear the chicken on high heat to lock in juices.
  4. Roast in oven for 25–30 mins or cook on low flame covered until tender.

Prep Time: 45 mins
Servings: 6

Biryani

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Fry onions until golden, keep half aside for garnish.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste, tomatoes, and chicken; cook until 80% done.
  3. Add yogurt, mint, coriander, biryani masala. Simmer.
  4. Parboil rice separately.

Prep Time: 50 mins
Servings: 8

Butter Chiken

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Cook chicken pieces (tandoori style or grilled with basic spices).
  2. In a pan, melt butter. Sauté ginger-garlic paste.
  3. Add tomato puree, cook until oil separates.
  4. Add chili powder, salt, garam masala.

Prep Time: 45 mins
Servings: 6

Chiken Fries

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with spices and a bit of lemon.
  2. Beat egg in one bowl, mix flours and seasoning in another
  3. Dip chicken in egg, then coat in flour mix.
  4. Heat oil and deep-fry in batches until golden and crispy.

Prep Time: 35 mins
Servings: 3
